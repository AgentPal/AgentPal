# Training Notes

## Audience

This training note is for a fictional sales operations lead, sales manager, or AI delivery reviewer who wants to understand how to use the Sales CRM Delivery Pack safely.

## How To Use The Pack

1. Start from `PROJECT.md` and confirm the business scenario is fictional or de-identified.
2. Read `PAL_TEAM.md` to understand candidate Pal perspectives.
3. Pick one flow from `FLOWS/`.
4. Use one task package from `TASKS/`.
5. Keep real CRM data in customer-private records.
6. Ask a human sales owner to review any customer-facing copy.
7. Ask a human reviewer to confirm compliance-sensitive claims.
8. Record missing evidence honestly.

## What Users Must Not Do

- Do not paste real CRM exports into this reusable pack.
- Do not store customer names, phone numbers, emails, or account IDs here.
- Do not add CRM tokens, cookies, webhooks, or API keys.
- Do not use this pack to send messages automatically.
- Do not treat candidate Pals as routes.

## Review Habits

- Treat segmentation as a recommendation until a human confirms it.
- Treat all generated copy as draft text.
- Keep sensitive customer history private.
- Preserve `not-run` when no runtime action or human review happened.
- Use fake examples for demos.
