# Candidate Pal Team Blueprint

## Boundary

Candidate Pals are AI judgement inputs only. They are not a route map, domain map, role map, deterministic assignment table, or central roster edit.

The current Brain / AI must still judge ownership from current user request, central roster, project context, delivery pack, risk, and available runtime evidence.

## Candidate Pals

| Candidate Pal | Why Faye may consider this Pal | Review boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Sales Strategist Pal | Designs sales motion, qualification criteria, pipeline stage logic, and deal review questions. | Human sales owner must approve customer-facing strategy. |
| CRM Ops Pal | Organizes fields, lifecycle stages, manual import checks, and CRM hygiene notes. | No CRM connector, API client, token, or automatic sync. |
| Copywriting Pal | Drafts follow-up messages, objection-handling variants, and concise sales notes. | Human review required before sending. |
| Data Review Pal | Reviews segmentation evidence, missing fields, and trend summaries from de-identified data. | `not-run` and missing evidence remain explicit. |
| Compliance QA Pal | Flags risky claims, sensitive data, regulated statements, and review obligations. | Does not provide final legal or compliance approval. |
| Mira | Intake, clarification, stage judgement, and coordination candidate. | Must still respect central roster and AI owner judgement. |
| PalSmith | Converts Faye blueprint into Pal team creation or Pal asset tasks when approved. | Does not auto-write central contacts or official Pal assets. |

## Collaboration Pattern

1. Faye receives the business goal and source boundaries.
2. Mira or Faye clarifies missing context.
3. Sales Strategist Pal drafts segmentation and deal-review criteria.
4. CRM Ops Pal shapes CRM field and manual handoff notes.
5. Copywriting Pal drafts message variants.
6. Data Review Pal checks evidence and missing fields.
7. Compliance QA Pal reviews high-risk text.
8. PalSmith receives the build request if a custom Pal team is needed.

No step is automatic execution.
