# Task 002: Follow-up Message Pack

## Goal

Draft public-safe follow-up message variants for a fictional or de-identified sales scenario.

## Inputs

```text
message_type:
segment:
interest_category:
known_context_summary:
forbidden_claims:
human_reviewer:
```

## Candidate Pals

- Copywriting Pal
- Sales Strategist Pal
- Compliance QA Pal

## Draft Variants

### Variant A: Concise

```text
Thanks for the interest. Based on the scenario you described, a short next step would be to confirm the priority use case and decide whether a demo is useful.
```

### Variant B: Consultative

```text
It sounds like the team is comparing options and wants a clearer implementation path. A useful next step may be a short discovery call focused on goals, constraints, and success criteria.
```

### Variant C: Close-the-loop

```text
I wanted to close the loop on this thread. If the timing is not right, we can pause here and revisit when the use case becomes active again.
```

## Review Requirements

- Human review required before sending.
- Compliance QA review required for regulated claims.
- No real names, emails, phone numbers, deal amounts, or account details.
- Missing evidence must stay visible.

## Acceptance

Pass only when every draft is public-safe and marked as human-review required.
