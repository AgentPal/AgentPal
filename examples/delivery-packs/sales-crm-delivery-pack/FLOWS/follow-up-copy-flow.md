# Follow-up Copy Flow

## Purpose

Draft public-safe follow-up message variants for fictional or de-identified sales contexts.

## Message Types

- first response after inbound interest;
- demo follow-up;
- pricing clarification;
- inactive lead reactivation;
- polite close-the-loop note.

## Steps

1. Confirm the lead context is fictional or de-identified.
2. Choose the message type.
3. Draft concise copy variants.
4. Flag claims that need human proof.
5. Flag regulated or sensitive statements.
6. Send drafts to a human reviewer.

## Candidate Pals

- Copywriting Pal
- Sales Strategist Pal
- Compliance QA Pal

## Required Human Review

All customer-facing copy requires human review before use.

## Forbidden

Do not include real names, real emails, real phone numbers, real account facts, private commitments, or legal/compliance claims without review.
