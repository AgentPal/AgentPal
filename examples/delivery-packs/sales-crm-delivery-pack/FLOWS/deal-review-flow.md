# Deal Review Flow

## Purpose

Create a public-safe deal review structure for fictional or de-identified sales opportunities.

## Review Questions

- What is the current stage?
- What is the customer need category?
- What decision evidence is missing?
- What objection or risk needs review?
- What claim must be verified before customer communication?
- What is the next human-owned action?

## Steps

1. Gather de-identified opportunity summary.
2. Identify missing evidence.
3. Draft deal review questions.
4. Flag compliance, privacy, or claim risk.
5. Recommend next action for human owner review.
6. Record review status as `not-run`, `partial`, or `requires-human-review` until completed.

## Candidate Pals

- Sales Strategist Pal
- Data Review Pal
- Compliance QA Pal

## Boundary

This flow does not forecast real revenue, update CRM records, or make final sales decisions.
