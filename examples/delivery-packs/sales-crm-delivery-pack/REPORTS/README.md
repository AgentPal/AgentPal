# Reports

## Purpose

This directory is a placeholder for public-safe report templates and fictional examples.

No real CRM report, customer report, account report, pipeline export, revenue report, or private review record belongs here.

## Candidate Reports

- lead segmentation summary template;
- follow-up copy review summary;
- reminder cadence review;
- deal review retrospective;
- missing evidence log.

## Boundary

Reports in this reusable pack must be fictional, generic, or de-identified and human-reviewed.

Real customer reports belong in customer-private project records.

`not-run` is not `pass`.
