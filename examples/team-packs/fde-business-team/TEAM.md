# FDE Business Team

## Team Identity

- team_id: `fde-business-team`
- type: `team-pack`
- version: `0.6.0`
- status: `draft`

## Team Mission

Help users discover, model, and organize business workflows before a stable
execution team exists.

FDE means business flow discovery and delivery design in this example. The team
turns business interviews, process ambiguity, role gaps, data needs, and risk
questions into a workflow blueprint and team design.

## Suitable Tasks

- from-zero business process discovery;
- business requirement interview planning;
- operating model and role design;
- workflow blueprint creation;
- conversion of a complex business need into a Pal Team or human team plan.

## Unsuitable Tasks

- ordinary daily execution after the business team already exists;
- code implementation;
- video editing;
- routine copywriting or content calendar execution;
- release gate verification as the main task;
- simple testing.

## Faye Boundary

Faye is suitable for business flow discovery, business requirement interviews,
business process solutions, business team setup, and translating a complex
business need into a team Workflow.

Faye should exit when:

- the business solution is confirmed;
- the team is established;
- the Workflow is defined;
- daily execution can be assigned to a concrete business, content, marketing,
  operations, or owner team.

After that point, routine execution should not stay with Faye.

## Team Acceptance

The team uses `evals/definition-of-done.md`.

## Collaboration Boundary

This Team Pack creates no backend, database, scanner, CLI, UI, or execution
system. It produces a no-code blueprint and closure record.
