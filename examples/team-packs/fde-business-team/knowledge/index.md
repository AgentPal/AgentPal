# FDE Business Team Knowledge Index

## Shared Knowledge

- FDE work starts when the process is unclear, not when a stable execution team already exists.
- A business blueprint should state current process, target process, roles, inputs, outputs, decisions, risks, and handoff rules.
- Faye is a discovery and setup owner, not a default operator for all business tasks.
- Team design must include exit conditions and post-handoff owners.
- Daily execution belongs to the appropriate business, content, marketing, operations, or project team.

## Public Boundary

Do not store confidential business interviews, customer lists, revenue data,
contracts, credentials, or private operating records in this public example.
