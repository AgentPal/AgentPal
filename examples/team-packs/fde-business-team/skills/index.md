# FDE Business Team Skills Index

## Available Team Methods

- `business-interview-framing`: prepare questions for discovering process goals, constraints, and stakeholders.
- `process-map-outline`: turn interview notes into current and target flow diagrams or text maps.
- `role-and-data-inventory`: identify owners, inputs, outputs, data dependencies, and handoff points.
- `workflow-blueprint-review`: check whether the workflow is ready to hand off to an execution team.

## Runtime Boundary

These are no-code planning methods. They do not create databases, automations,
project-management systems, dashboards, or runtime services.
