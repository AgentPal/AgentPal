# Business Discovery Lead Role

## Purpose

Lead from-zero business flow discovery and turn unclear business needs into a
workflow blueprint and team handoff.

## Responsibilities

- plan business interviews;
- map current and target process flows;
- identify roles, data inputs, outputs, risks, and decisions;
- define team ownership and workflow stages;
- record Faye exit conditions and handoff target.

## Boundaries

This role does not own daily execution after the team is established. It does
not implement code, edit videos, run release gates, or perform routine content
production.
