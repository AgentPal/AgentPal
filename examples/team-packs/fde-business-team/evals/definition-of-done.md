# FDE Business Team Definition Of Done

The team's output is acceptable when:

- the business goal and discovery scope are explicit;
- current and target process are described;
- stakeholders, roles, inputs, outputs, data dependencies, and decisions are listed;
- risks, assumptions, and privacy boundaries are stated;
- team handoff and execution owner are identified or marked as missing;
- Faye exit conditions are recorded;
- routine daily execution is not left with Faye by default;
- verifier output is included when risk is medium or high.
