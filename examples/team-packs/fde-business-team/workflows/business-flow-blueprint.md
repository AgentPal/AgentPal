# Business Flow Blueprint Workflow

## Purpose

Turn an unclear business need into a workflow blueprint, role design, data list,
risk list, and execution-team handoff.

## Use When

- the user is starting from business ambiguity;
- the team, workflow, or operating model is not established;
- interviews, process mapping, and role design are needed.

## Do Not Use When

- an execution team already owns the recurring work;
- the task is routine content production, code implementation, video editing, or release verification;
- Faye would remain owner after the workflow is defined.

## Steps

1. Confirm discovery scope and whether daily execution already has an owner.
2. Plan business interviews and collect process constraints.
3. Map current process, target process, roles, data, and decisions.
4. Design the team handoff and Workflow blueprint.
5. Review risks, assumptions, acceptance criteria, and Faye exit condition.
6. Produce final blueprint and writeback decision.
