# FDE Business Team Runbook Index

## Business Flow Blueprint Procedure

1. Confirm whether the task is from-zero discovery or daily execution.
2. Identify stakeholders, business goal, current process, and pain points.
3. Map target workflow, roles, data inputs, outputs, and decisions.
4. Design the execution team or Pal Team handoff.
5. Review assumptions, risks, privacy boundaries, and acceptance criteria.
6. Record Faye exit conditions and next owner.

## Exception Handling

- If the user asks for routine execution after a team exists, veto Faye and hand off to the execution owner or ask the user for that team.
- If private business data is required, keep the public example clean and mark the needed private context separately.
- If the workflow needs implementation, create a separate owner judgement and Task Package.
