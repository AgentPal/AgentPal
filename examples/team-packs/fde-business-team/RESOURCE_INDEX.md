# FDE Business Team Resource Index

## Root Files

- `TEAM.md`: team identity, Faye boundary, and public boundary.
- `team.json`: machine-readable Team Pack manifest.
- `roster.json`: Pal references and open execution-team roles.
- `RESOURCE_INDEX.md`: quick navigation.

## Team Assets

- `roles/business-discovery-lead.md`: FDE discovery role card.
- `knowledge/index.md`: shared business flow discovery knowledge.
- `skills/index.md`: team methods for interviews, process maps, and role design.
- `workflows/business-flow-blueprint.md`: representative workflow.
- `runbooks/index.md`: repeatable FDE procedure.
- `memory/team-memory.md`: public-safe team memory policy.
- `evals/definition-of-done.md`: quality and exit gate.
- `routing/team-routing-card.json`: team selection and veto guidance.
- `reports/business-flow-blueprint-e2e-simulation.md`: read-only simulation report.

## Boundary

This Team Pack helps create the business workflow and team handoff. It is not
the execution team for recurring business operations.
