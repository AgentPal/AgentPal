# Marketing Growth Team

## Team Identity

- team_id: `marketing-growth-team`
- type: `team-pack`
- version: `0.6.0`
- status: `draft`

## Team Mission

Help AgentPal plan and deliver public-facing promotion work: content themes,
short-form copy, visual briefs, publishing plans, and performance retrospectives.

This Team Pack is a no-code planning and coordination asset. It does not publish
content, upload media, run ad accounts, operate analytics systems, or create a
background marketing service.

## Suitable Tasks

- content theme planning for AgentPal;
- weekly promotion content calendars;
- short-form promotion copy and channel adaptation;
- visual requirement briefs for designers or image tools;
- publishing schedule planning;
- post-campaign review and learning candidates.

## Unsuitable Tasks

- automatic content publication;
- paid ad operations or account management;
- business process discovery as the primary task;
- direct code implementation;
- final release verification;
- private customer-data analysis.

## PalSmith Creation Decision

PalSmith is needed for Team Pack creation and governance review. PalSmith is not
a daily promotion executor after this Team Pack exists.

No new Pal is created in this example. Visual design and publishing operations
remain open roles until the user confirms whether they want new named Pals or
external runtime/human operators.

## Faye Boundary

Faye is not a routine member of this team. Faye may be consulted only if the
promotion workflow itself needs business process discovery or redesign.
