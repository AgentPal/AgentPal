# Marketing Growth Team Definition Of Done

The team's output is acceptable when:

- campaign goal, audience, channel, and success signal are stated;
- each content item has a topic, rationale, copy direction, visual requirement, and publishing note;
- current AgentPal capability claims are not overstated;
- future design is labeled as future design;
- visual requirements are separate from actual design execution;
- publication and analytics are not claimed without evidence;
- Quinn review is present when risk or public-facing claim complexity warrants it, or verification is legally skipped with a reason;
- memory and routing writeback decisions are explicit.
