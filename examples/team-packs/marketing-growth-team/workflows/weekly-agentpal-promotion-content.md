# Weekly AgentPal Promotion Content Workflow

## Purpose

Design a weekly set of AgentPal promotion content items with topic rationale,
copy direction, visual requirements, publishing plan, and retrospective hook.

## Use When

- the user asks for a weekly promotion plan;
- the task needs content themes, copy, visuals, schedule, and review;
- publication will be handled later by a human or authorized runtime.

## Do Not Use When

- the user asks for business process discovery before the promotion team exists;
- the request is actual platform publishing without authorization or evidence;
- the task is release verification, code work, or private analytics processing.

## Steps

1. Clarify promotion objective, audience, channel, and success signal.
2. Select content themes and topic rationale.
3. Generate content items and copy direction.
4. Generate visual requirements for each item.
5. Review public claim risk and brand consistency.
6. Prepare publishing schedule and owner handoff.
7. Plan post-publication retrospective and writeback decision.

## Workflow Execution Contract Shape

| Step ID | Owner Role | Owner Pal / Open Role | Output Contract | Verifier | Closure Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| M1 | team-conductor | Mira | campaign goal, audience, channels, constraints | none | must close |
| M2 | positioning-lead | Nova | message strategy, topic ladder, success signal | Quinn if high risk | must close |
| M3 | research-support | Vega | evidence or trend notes for topic claims | Quinn if needed | must close or legally skip |
| M4 | copy-lead | Harper | five content drafts or copy briefs | Quinn if medium/high risk | must close |
| M5 | visual-brief-owner | open role | visual requirements for each content item | none | must close or be blocked/skipped with reason |
| M6 | quality-reviewer | Quinn | risk and completion review when needed | none | must close or legally skip |
| M7 | publishing-operator | open role | publishing plan and external execution boundary | none | must close or be blocked/skipped with reason |
| M8 | team-conductor | Mira | final report, retrospective hook, writeback decision | none | must close |

## Output Contract

- campaign goal and audience;
- five content topics with rationale;
- copy direction or draft text for each item;
- visual requirement brief for each item;
- publishing schedule and owner handoff;
- risk / claim review status;
- retrospective and memory writeback decision.
