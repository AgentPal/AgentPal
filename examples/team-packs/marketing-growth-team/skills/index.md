# Marketing Growth Team Skills Index

Team-level Skills describe shared methods. They do not replace Pal-owned Skills
or runtime plugins.

## Available Team Methods

- `campaign-goal-framing`: define audience, channel, goal, and success signal.
- `topic-ladder`: turn a campaign goal into topic clusters and weekly content items.
- `promotion-copy-brief`: draft channel-ready copy with claim and tone notes.
- `visual-requirement-brief`: convert content intent into visual requirements.
- `publishing-plan`: prepare a schedule and owner list without claiming publication.
- `retrospective-summary`: capture results, lessons, and memory candidates.

## Runtime Boundary

This Team Pack does not call social platforms, analytics APIs, image tools, or
publishing tools by itself.
