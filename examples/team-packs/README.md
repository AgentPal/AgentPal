# Team Pack Examples

This directory contains public-safe Team Pack examples for AgentPal.

Examples demonstrate structure and boundaries. They are not customer data, not
runtime engines, not route maps, and not full business systems.

They are no-code examples. End-to-end reports in these folders are read-only
simulations unless they explicitly say a real runtime session was run.

## Official Examples

- `research-team/`: source-grounded research team with evidence review and a read-only brief simulation.
- `video-production-team/`: video planning team for scripts, visual direction, production handoff, and readiness review.
- `fde-business-team/`: business flow discovery team for process blueprinting, team setup, and Faye handoff boundaries.
- `marketing-growth-team/`: promotion team for AgentPal content topics, copy, visual briefs, publishing plans, and retrospectives.

Each example includes Team Pack root files, shared assets, a representative
workflow, routing guidance, a definition of done, and an end-to-end simulation
report.

## How To Read An Example

1. Open `TEAM.md` to understand the mission and boundaries.
2. Open `roster.json` to see registered Pals and open roles.
3. Open `workflows/` to see the repeatable team process.
4. Open `evals/definition-of-done.md` to see what acceptable output means.
5. Open `reports/` to see no-code rehearsal reports and remaining validation gaps.

If you want to create a new Team Pack, start with the
[creation guide](../../docs/03-pal-pack-standard/18-team-pack-creation-guide.md)
and then use the
[validation checklist](../../docs/03-pal-pack-standard/19-team-pack-validation-checklist.md).
