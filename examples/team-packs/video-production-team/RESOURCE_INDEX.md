# Video Production Team Resource Index

## Root Files

- `TEAM.md`: team identity and public boundary.
- `team.json`: machine-readable Team Pack manifest.
- `roster.json`: Pal references and open production role.
- `RESOURCE_INDEX.md`: quick navigation.

## Team Assets

- `roles/video-coordinator.md`: lead coordination role.
- `knowledge/index.md`: shared video planning knowledge.
- `skills/index.md`: team-level methods for scripts and production briefs.
- `workflows/video-production-brief.md`: representative workflow.
- `runbooks/index.md`: repeatable production planning procedure.
- `memory/team-memory.md`: public-safe memory policy.
- `evals/definition-of-done.md`: team quality gate.
- `routing/team-routing-card.json`: AI judgement input for team selection.
- `reports/video-production-brief-e2e-simulation.md`: read-only simulation report.

## Boundary

This Team Pack can plan and review video production. It does not render videos,
edit footage, upload media, or provide a background production system.
