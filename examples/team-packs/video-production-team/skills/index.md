# Video Production Team Skills Index

## Available Team Methods

- `video-goal-framing`: define objective, audience, platform, and conversion target.
- `script-structure`: turn a message into hook, body, proof, and call-to-action.
- `visual-direction-outline`: describe scenes, pacing, style, and asset needs.
- `production-readiness-review`: check whether the plan can be handed to an editor or runtime.

## Runtime Boundary

These are planning methods. They do not call a video editor, renderer, upload tool, or media pipeline.
