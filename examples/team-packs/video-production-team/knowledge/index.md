# Video Production Team Knowledge Index

## Shared Knowledge

- A production plan must state audience, format, duration, platform, and target action.
- A script must be reviewable before any rendering or editing is claimed.
- Visual direction should include pacing, scene structure, asset needs, and accessibility notes.
- Factual claims in a video require source notes or must be marked as unverified.
- Real editing, rendering, upload, and publication are execution-layer actions.

## Public Boundary

Do not store private footage, raw customer assets, licensed media, voice files,
or unpublished campaign material in this public example.
