# Video Production Brief Workflow

## Purpose

Create a reviewable video production brief that can be handed to a human editor
or an authorized runtime tool.

## Use When

- the user asks for a video plan, script, storyboard, or production handoff;
- audience, message, visual direction, and review all matter;
- actual editing may happen later but is not part of the Team Pack itself.

## Steps

1. Confirm objective, audience, platform, duration, and tone.
2. Define message strategy and proof needs.
3. Draft script and scene sequence.
4. Specify visual direction, asset list, and accessibility notes.
5. Review claims and production readiness.
6. Produce final production brief and publishing checklist.

## Output Contract

- production objective and constraints;
- script or storyboard outline;
- visual direction and asset list;
- source or claim notes when relevant;
- review notes and readiness status;
- handoff instructions for human editor or runtime.
