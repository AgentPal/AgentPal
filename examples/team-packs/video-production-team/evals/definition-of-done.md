# Video Production Team Definition Of Done

The team's output is acceptable when:

- the goal, audience, format, platform, and duration are stated;
- the script or outline matches the stated goal;
- visual direction and asset needs are clear;
- factual claims are sourced, marked unverified, or removed;
- actual editing, rendering, and publication are not claimed without execution evidence;
- verifier output is included when risk is medium or high;
- the final handoff is usable by a human editor or authorized runtime.
