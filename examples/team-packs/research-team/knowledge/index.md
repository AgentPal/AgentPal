# Research Team Knowledge Index

This Team Pack uses shared knowledge for source-grounded research tasks.

## Current Shared Knowledge

- Research questions must be explicit before source synthesis begins.
- Source access must be stated as current evidence, user-provided material, or unavailable.
- Facts, inferences, recommendations, and uncertainty must be separated.
- Weak evidence should stay marked as weak evidence.
- Runtime search, browsing, PDF extraction, or file inspection requires current host evidence.

## Public Boundary

Do not store private user research notes, confidential customer data, paid database exports, or copied third-party articles in this public example.
