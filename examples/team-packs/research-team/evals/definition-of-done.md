# Research Team Definition Of Done

Research Team output is acceptable when:

- the research question and decision use are explicit;
- source availability and freshness are stated;
- unsupported claims are flagged;
- facts, inferences, recommendations, and uncertainty are separated;
- findings are tied to source notes or clearly marked as inference;
- next research gaps are listed;
- verifier output is present when verification was required, or verification is legally skipped with a reason;
- no hard-coded Pal route is claimed.
