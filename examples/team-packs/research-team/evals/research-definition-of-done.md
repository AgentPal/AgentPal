# Research Definition Of Done

This file is retained as a compatibility alias. The current standard eval entry
is `definition-of-done.md`.

Research Team output is acceptable when:

- the research question is explicit;
- source availability is stated;
- unsupported claims are flagged;
- uncertainty is not hidden;
- findings are separated from recommendations;
- next research gaps are listed;
- no hard-coded Pal route is claimed.
