# Research Team Runbook Index

## Source-Grounded Brief Procedure

1. Confirm the research question and decision use.
2. Define allowed source scope and freshness needs.
3. Build a source inventory or mark source access as unavailable.
4. Synthesize findings with confidence and uncertainty labels.
5. Ask Quinn or another selected verifier to review evidence when risk is medium or high.
6. Close the Workflow Execution Contract before reporting completion.

## Exception Handling

- If source access is unavailable, mark the source collection Step as `blocked` or `skipped` with a reason.
- If verification is named but cannot run, do not claim review passed.
- If the user changes the research question mid-task, replan the affected Step.
