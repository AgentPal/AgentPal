# Research Team Resource Index

## Root Files

- `TEAM.md`: team identity and use policy.
- `team.json`: machine-readable Team Pack manifest.
- `roster.json`: Pal references and roles.
- `TEAM_CONTACTS.md`: readable roster summary.

## Key Assets

- `roles/research-coordinator.md`: lightweight role card.
- `knowledge/index.md`: shared research context boundaries.
- `skills/index.md`: team-level research methods.
- `runbooks/index.md`: repeatable source-grounded brief procedure.
- `memory/team-memory.md`: public-safe team memory policy and candidates.
- `workflows/source-grounded-brief.md`: representative team workflow.
- `evals/definition-of-done.md`: standard team definition of done.
- `evals/research-definition-of-done.md`: compatibility alias for the same eval.
- `routing/team-routing-card.json`: routing judgement card.
- `reports/source-grounded-brief-e2e-simulation.md`: read-only end-to-end simulation.

## Boundary

Use this example as a public-safe structure sample. Do not treat it as a
complete research runtime or hard-coded route table.
