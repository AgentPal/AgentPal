# Knowledge Index

## Purpose

This directory lists stable professional knowledge owned by `[Pal name]`.

Knowledge includes concepts, rules, domain context, reusable principles, verified references, and durable boundaries that help the Pal do its job.

## What Belongs Here

- Stable facts and rules.
- Domain concepts and terminology.
- Reusable principles.
- Reviewed research conclusions.
- De-identified examples that serve as knowledge references.
- Links to related research sources, Skills, workflows, runbooks, examples, and evals.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Raw source dumps that belong in `research/`.
- Current task state.
- Full reports that belong in `reports/`.
- Long-term memory records that belong in `memory/`.
- Work procedures that belong in `workflows/` or `runbooks/`.
- Credentials, tokens, secrets, private customer data, or unreviewed private material.
- Keyword route maps or deterministic dispatch rules.

## Review Notes

- Research becomes knowledge only after review and source-aware summarization.
- Mark uncertain or stale facts as `unknown` or `needs-review`.
- Keep private project facts in approved private project records, not public Pal knowledge.

## Related Standards

- `standards/pal-asset/pal-asset-standard.md`
- `standards/pal-asset/pal-asset-directory-standard.md`

## Public Safety Boundary

Do not include credentials, tokens, secrets, private user material, private project memory, or customer-private evidence.

## External Project Boundary

Knowledge assets belong in the central Pal Pack by default. External projects stay thin-bound and do not receive copied central knowledge by default.
