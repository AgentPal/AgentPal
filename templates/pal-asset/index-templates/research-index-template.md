# Research Index

## Purpose

This directory lists source gathering, source inventories, evidence extracts, comparison notes, research drafts, and coverage matrices owned by `[Pal name]`.

Research preserves source context before stable conclusions are promoted into knowledge or other assets.

## What Belongs Here

- Source inventories.
- Source coverage matrices.
- Evidence notes.
- Comparative research drafts.
- Public-safe source summaries.
- Research gap notes.
- Links to related knowledge, Skills, workflows, examples, evals, and reports.

## What Does Not Belong Here

- Final stable rules without source context.
- Long-term memory.
- Runtime state.
- Full completion reports.
- Private paid content copied without permission.
- Credentials, tokens, secrets, private customer data, or private project evidence.
- Keyword routing maps or deterministic dispatch tables.

## Review Notes

- Promote research conclusions to `knowledge/` only after review.
- Preserve `unknown`, `not-run`, and `needs-review` for unverified source claims.
- Keep copyright, privacy, and source-license limits visible.

## Public Safety Boundary

Do not include private sources, credentials, tokens, secrets, customer identifiers, or copyrighted source excerpts beyond allowed use.

## External Project Boundary

Research assets belong in central Pal assets or approved central research records by default. Do not write them into external project `.agentpal/` by default.
