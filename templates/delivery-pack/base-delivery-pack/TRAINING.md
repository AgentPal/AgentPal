# Training

## Purpose

Explain how users should operate the Delivery Pack after human review.

## User Orientation

- what this delivery pack is for;
- what information users must provide;
- how Flow Packs and Task Packages are selected;
- what Faye prepares;
- what PalSmith creates or completes;
- where private project records belong.

## Operating Rules

- keep external projects thin-bound;
- keep customer-private evidence out of reusable packs;
- preserve `missing`, `not-run`, and `requires-human-review`;
- request human review before high-risk use;
- do not claim runtime execution without evidence.

## Escalation

Escalate when credentials, private customer data, high-risk professional advice, missing source evidence, or external system access is required.
