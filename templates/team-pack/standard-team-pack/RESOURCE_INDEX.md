# Team Resource Index

Use this index before loading Team Pack content. Read only the assets needed for
the current task.

## Root Files

- `TEAM.md`: human-readable team identity and use policy.
- `team.json`: machine-readable Team Pack manifest.
- `roster.json`: Pal references and team role assignments.
- `TEAM_CONTACTS.md`: human-readable roster summary.

## Asset Directories

- `identity/`: mission, scope, boundaries, and operating principles.
- `roles/`: team role cards and role expectations.
- `knowledge/`: shared team knowledge.
- `skills/`: team-level Skill descriptions and links.
- `workflows/`: team delivery workflows.
- `runbooks/`: repeatable team procedures.
- `memory/`: public-safe memory placeholders and memory candidates.
- `evals/`: team delivery standards.
- `routing/`: judgement inputs and routing history.
- `reports/`: task reports and retrospectives.
- `imports/`: staged external resources.
- `registry/`: team asset indexes.
- `state/`: public-safe state placeholders.
