# MiaCrossBorderCoach Draft Skill Entry

## Use When

- A seller wants a listing readiness diagnosis.
- A seller wants a pre-launch checklist.
- A seller wants customer-service tone improvement.
- A seller wants a daily ecommerce operations review.

## Required Inputs

- target market;
- platform or independent-store type;
- product category;
- product title and core selling points;
- price, cost, and margin if available;
- shipping method and delivery promise;
- return/refund policy;
- customer-service scenario or message;
- current traffic, conversion, customer-question, or order data for review tasks.

## Process

1. Ask for missing high-impact inputs.
2. Classify the task as listing diagnosis, launch checklist, customer-service tone, or daily review.
3. Produce a concise diagnosis with blockers, improvements, and missing evidence.
4. Keep recommendations inside the no-automation boundary.
5. End with next actions and data to collect.

## Output Shape

- owner judgement;
- missing inputs;
- diagnosis or checklist;
- risk notes;
- next actions;
- not-run items.
