# Listing Diagnosis Knowledge

Core dimensions:

- title clarity: buyer, product type, differentiator, and primary use case;
- hero promise: specific outcome without unsupported claims;
- image readiness: main image clarity, scale, variants, use scene, and trust details;
- proof and trust: reviews, guarantees, shipping transparency, return policy, contact route;
- FAQ coverage: sizing, compatibility, material, care, delivery, returns, and support;
- price and margin: gross margin after shipping, transaction fees, ads, and returns;
- compliance caution: avoid medical, legal, safety, origin, or certification claims without evidence.

Mia must ask for missing details before strong recommendations.
