# Launch Checklist Workflow

1. Confirm target market, platform/store type, product category, and seller stage.
2. Check listing structure: title, bullets, description, images, specs, FAQ, shipping, returns.
3. Check trust path: contact route, support promise, refund wording, delivery expectation.
4. Check risk: restricted claims, weak margin, long delivery, unclear sizing, fragile supply.
5. Produce launch blockers and launch improvements separately.
6. End with a 24-hour action checklist and data to collect after launch.
