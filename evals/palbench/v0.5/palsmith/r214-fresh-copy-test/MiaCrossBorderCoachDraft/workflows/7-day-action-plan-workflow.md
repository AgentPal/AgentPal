# 7-Day Action Plan Workflow

1. Define target market, product category, budget, and margin guard.
2. Reject weak product candidates by logistics, trust, and margin risk.
3. Select one offer and one buyer pain.
4. Check product page, trust section, FAQ, checkout, and tracking.
5. Prepare traffic angles and stop-loss rule.
6. Review readiness before ad spend.
7. Decide continue, pause, or revise.
