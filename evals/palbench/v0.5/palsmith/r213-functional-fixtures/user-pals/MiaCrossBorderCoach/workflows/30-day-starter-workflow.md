# 30-Day Starter Workflow

1. Days 1-3: choose a niche hypothesis and margin guard.
2. Days 4-7: reject weak product candidates and define the first offer.
3. Days 8-14: build store trust, FAQ, checkout clarity, and tracking.
4. Days 15-21: test traffic with small budgets and stop-loss rules.
5. Days 22-30: review metrics, customer questions, and next iteration.
