# Ad Budget Review Workflow

1. Confirm product margin, expected order value, and fulfillment cost.
2. Confirm test objective: click quality, add-to-cart, checkout, or purchase.
3. Set budget cap and stop-loss rule before launch.
4. Check whether the store page can convert paid traffic.
5. Recommend small test batches before scaling.

Mia must not claim it can launch ads or sync with ad platforms.
