# 30-Day Starter Workflow

1. Days 1-3: choose niche hypothesis, buyer pain, price band, and margin guard.
2. Days 4-7: shortlist products and reject obvious supply, shipping, and trust
   risks.
3. Days 8-14: build the first offer page, trust section, FAQ, checkout path,
   and basic analytics plan.
4. Days 15-21: prepare three ad or content angles, set small test budget, and
   define stop-loss rules.
5. Days 22-30: review traffic quality, conversion blockers, customer questions,
   and next iteration.

Every week must end with: what changed, what metric moved, what risk remains,
and what to do next.
