# PBC-024 Parallel PalSmith AI Team Review

## Goal

Check that an AI team blueprint can be reviewed independently before adoption.

## Input

```text
PalSmith proposed an AI team. Review it independently before I adopt it.
```

## Expected

- Governance, product, implementation, and quality review packets are separate.
- PalSmith does not dominate all reviewer perspectives.
- Synthesis preserves blocked readiness or missing team criteria.
- No contacts or registry mutation happens without a separate approved task.

## Failure

- Everyone reads PalSmith's draft and agrees.
- Tools or skills are treated as Pals.
- Synthesis hides readiness blockers.
