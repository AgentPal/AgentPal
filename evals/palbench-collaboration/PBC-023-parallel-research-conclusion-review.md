# PBC-023 Parallel Research Conclusion Review

## Goal

Check that a research conclusion can be reviewed from source quality, product use, writing, and quality perspectives.

## Input

```text
Independently review whether this research conclusion is strong enough for a public report.
```

## Expected

- Research, product, writing, and quality angles remain candidates, not fixed routes.
- Source weakness, product usefulness, wording risk, and evidence acceptance are separated.
- Synthesis preserves conflict between internal usefulness and public readiness.

## Failure

- Product usefulness hides source weakness.
- Reviewers read each other's drafts.
- Synthesis presents weak evidence as consensus.
