# PalSmith Black And White Avatar Logo Brief

## Design Goal

Create a black and white avatar/logo for PalSmith that can work as:

- website logo mark;
- desktop app icon;
- docs/avatar image;
- small navigation icon;
- product identity symbol for AgentPal's Pal asset governance layer.

## Concept

The mark combines three ideas:

- shield: governance, boundary, public-safety review;
- forge/anvil: "Smith" as a maker and refiner of Pal assets;
- check mark: readiness, verification, and controlled approval.

The result should feel like a quiet product seal, not a mascot and not a generic robot.

## Visual Direction

Style: strict black and white, geometric, compact, high contrast.

Shape language:

- shield silhouette for trust and governance;
- stacked bars for Pal Pack assets / structured files;
- forge base for craft and repair;
- check mark for quality gate and readiness.

## Usage

Use `palsmith-logo-mark.svg` on light backgrounds.

Use `palsmith-app-icon.svg` for:

- desktop app icon;
- square avatar;
- favicon source;
- launcher tile;
- dark social preview.

## Small-size Rule

At sizes under 32px, use the app icon version. Avoid adding text next to the mark in tiny contexts.

## What This Logo Should Not Become

- not a humanoid face;
- not a hammer-only blacksmith icon;
- not a scanner / validator / CLI symbol;
- not a detailed illustration;
- not a colorful mascot;
- not a decorative tech gradient.

## Design Rationale

PalSmith is a no-code Pal asset governor. Its logo should signal judgement, craft, review, and safe packaging. The black and white treatment keeps it authoritative and durable across websites, app shells, documentation, and monochrome UI surfaces.
