# PalSmith Character Avatar Brief

## Direction

PalSmith should use a black and white character avatar as the primary product-facing logo direction.

The avatar should feel like a calm Pal asset governor: precise, careful, craft-oriented, and quietly authoritative. It should be closer to a modern AI agent portrait mark than an abstract seal.

## Character Concept

PalSmith is visualized as an androgynous young craftsperson / archivist / asset governor:

- sharp but calm eyes;
- clean short hair with a strong silhouette;
- high-collar work jacket or subtle smith apron;
- a small chest badge combining stacked assets and a verification check;
- no robot features;
- no mascot exaggeration;
- no direct resemblance to another agent logo.

## Why This Fits PalSmith

PalSmith's job is to create, inspect, repair, govern, and publish-gate Pal assets. The portrait gives the product a human-facing identity, while the badge carries the governance signal:

- stacked cards: Pal Pack assets;
- check mark: readiness and verification;
- clean black/white portrait: authority, restraint, and durability;
- workwear collar / apron: "Smith" as craft, not automation.

## Usage

Use `palsmith-character-avatar.png` for:

- website hero or product navigation avatar;
- desktop app icon candidate;
- docs and Pal profile avatar;
- social preview image;
- onboarding or team roster surfaces.

Use the existing abstract SVG mark only as a secondary small symbol when a portrait is too detailed.

## Style Rules

- black and white only;
- high contrast;
- strong silhouette;
- clear face and badge at medium size;
- avoid thin details that vanish below 64px;
- keep the circular frame for avatar use;
- keep the face original and not tied to any public figure, anime character, or third-party agent.

## Do Not Do

- do not copy Hermes Agent's exact logo, pose, face, hair, or framing;
- do not use letters or text inside the avatar;
- do not make PalSmith look like a generic robot assistant;
- do not use colorful gradients;
- do not make the image overly cute, childish, or decorative;
- do not imply this is an official external-agent logo.
