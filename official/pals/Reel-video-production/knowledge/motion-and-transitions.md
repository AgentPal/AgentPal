# Motion And Transitions

## Motion Roles

- Direct attention.
- Reveal structure.
- Create continuity between scenes.
- Add emotional energy.
- Explain change over time.

## Transition Types

- Cut.
- Crossfade.
- Wipe.
- Push.
- Match cut.
- Text-driven transition.
- Product UI zoom or pan.
- Shader or stylized transition.

## Reel Rules

- A transition should explain the relationship between two beats.
- Fast transitions need simple frames.
- Complex frames need slower transitions.
- In HyperFrames, multi-scene compositions should use transitions and entrance animations according to the HyperFrames rules.
