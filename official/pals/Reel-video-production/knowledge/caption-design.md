# Caption Design

## Caption Roles

- Accessibility transcript.
- Emphasis and memory.
- Silent-autoplay comprehension.
- Step labels.
- Platform-native visual style.

## Rules

- Keep captions short enough to read before they vanish.
- Do not cover key product UI.
- Use consistent placement unless the shot requires movement.
- Highlight keywords sparingly.
- Check contrast and safe areas.
- Use word-level timing only when the rhythm benefits from it.

## Failure Modes

- Full transcript captions that cover the video.
- Captions out of sync with speech.
- Too many styles in one video.
- Captions below platform UI safe area.
