# Voiceover And Sound Design

## Voiceover

- Match pace to information density.
- Use pauses to make concepts land.
- Avoid overlong sentences in short videos.
- Check pronunciation for product names, commands, and technical terms.

## Sound Design

- Music supports energy, not volume.
- Sound effects should mark transitions, confirmations, or attention shifts.
- Duck music under voice.
- Avoid busy sounds under dense captions.

## Tool Notes

TTS, transcription, audio cleanup, and loudness checks are runtime candidates. Reel must mark them `not-run` unless actual tool evidence exists.
