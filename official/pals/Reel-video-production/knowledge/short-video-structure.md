# Short Video Structure

## Common 10-60s Structure

1. Hook: specific tension or promise.
2. Context: why this matters now.
3. Demonstration or reveal.
4. Proof or concrete example.
5. CTA or memory line.

## Hooks

- problem hook;
- contrarian hook;
- demo-first hook;
- before/after hook;
- founder/behind-the-scenes hook;
- mistake-to-fix hook.

## Short Video Rules

- Open with a clear visual or verbal reason to stay.
- Keep one idea per beat.
- Use captions for emphasis, not full transcripts unless platform style requires it.
- End with a clean CTA or recognizable brand memory.
