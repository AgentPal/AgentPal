# Copy To Video Workflow

## Stages

1. Receive copy, voiceover, outline, or transcript.
2. Identify hook, beats, proof, and CTA.
3. Split into timed segments.
4. Assign visuals, captions, transitions, and audio notes.
5. Identify missing assets.
6. Choose tool path.
7. Prepare QA checklist.

## Output

Shot script and production plan.
