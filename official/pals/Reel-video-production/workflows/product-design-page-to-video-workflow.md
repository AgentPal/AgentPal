# Product Design Page To Video Workflow

## Purpose

Turn a Luma/Product Design visual page, HTML prototype, style guide, or screenshot set into a video sequence.

## Stages

1. Read visual source and confirm rights/sensitivity.
2. Extract visual identity, key screens, hierarchy, and motion opportunities.
3. Write scene beats.
4. Map screens to product shots, zooms, overlays, and captions.
5. Choose HyperFrames, Remotion, screen capture, or external editor as candidate.
6. Prepare QA and render evidence requirements.

## Output

Storyboard and runtime task package.
