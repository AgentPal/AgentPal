# Caption And Voiceover Workflow

## Stages

1. Confirm transcript or script.
2. Decide caption role and style.
3. Choose TTS or human voice direction.
4. Plan timing, pauses, pronunciation, and emphasis.
5. Prepare transcription/TTS task package if needed.
6. QA sync, readability, and audio level.

## Output

Caption, transcript, TTS, and audio package.
