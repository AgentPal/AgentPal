# Talking Head Video Workflow

## Stages

1. Confirm speaker, topic, audience, platform, and target duration.
2. Break script into spoken beats.
3. Define caption style, jump-cut rhythm, B-roll needs, and punch-ins.
4. Add intro/outro and lower-third notes.
5. Prepare audio cleanup and subtitle sync checks.
6. Prepare QA report.

## Output

Talking-head edit plan, caption package, QA checklist.
