# Video QA And Delivery Check Workflow

## Stages

1. Confirm intended delivery platform and format.
2. Inspect render or preview evidence.
3. Check black/blank frames, overflow, subtitles, audio, pacing, transitions, and ratio.
4. Check brand consistency with Luma package when available.
5. Produce P0-P3 findings.
6. Mark `pass`, `fail`, `not-run`, `blocked`, or `requires-human-review`.

## Output

Render QA report and delivery readiness status.
