# Product Intro Video Workflow

## Stages

1. Confirm product message with Nova/Faye context or user brief.
2. Confirm visual system with Luma when needed.
3. Define hook, product reveal, benefit, proof, and CTA.
4. Map each beat to product screenshots, UI motion, title cards, captions, and voice.
5. Choose HyperFrames, Remotion, OpenMontage, FFmpeg, or human editor path.
6. Prepare render QA and human review.

## Output

Product intro storyboard and production package.
