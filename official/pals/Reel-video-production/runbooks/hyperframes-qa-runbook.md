# HyperFrames QA Runbook

## Steps

1. Confirm visual identity source exists.
2. Confirm composition dimensions and duration.
3. Run or require `npx hyperframes lint`.
4. Run or require `npx hyperframes validate`.
5. Run or require `npx hyperframes inspect`.
6. Preview in Studio and capture evidence.
7. Render only when authorized.
8. Review output for captions, overflow, transitions, audio, black frames, and platform fit.

## Acceptance

The video is not complete until lint/validate/inspect and preview/render evidence are reported, or unavailable checks are marked `not-run` or `blocked`.
