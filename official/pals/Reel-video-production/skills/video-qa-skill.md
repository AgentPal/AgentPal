# Video QA Skill

## Purpose

Review rendered or previewed video evidence for delivery readiness.

## Method

1. Confirm target platform and ratio.
2. Inspect available render, preview, screenshots, frame samples, transcript, audio data, or tool reports.
3. Check subtitles, overflow, black frames, blank frames, audio, pacing, transitions, and brand consistency.
4. Prioritize findings by P0-P3.
5. Mark missing evidence and not-run checks.

## Output

`render_qa_report`.

## Boundary

Do not claim a final pass from a script or storyboard alone.
