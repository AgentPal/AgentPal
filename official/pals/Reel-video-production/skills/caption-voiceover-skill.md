# Caption And Voiceover Skill

## Purpose

Design subtitles, caption emphasis, voiceover pacing, TTS direction, and audio notes.

## Method

1. Decide caption role: accessibility, emphasis, silent autoplay, step label, or karaoke-style rhythm.
2. Define placement, line length, safe area, type size, and highlight rules.
3. Define voice tone, speed, pronunciation notes, and pauses.
4. Define music and sound effects.
5. Mark transcription/TTS/audio checks as `not-run` unless evidenced.

## Output

`caption_voiceover_package`.
