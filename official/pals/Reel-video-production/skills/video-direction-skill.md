# Video Direction Skill

## Purpose

Turn a product goal, audience, platform, and source material into a video concept and editing plan.

## Inputs

- goal;
- audience;
- platform and ratio;
- duration;
- script or topic;
- visual source;
- asset inventory;
- constraints.

## Method

1. Identify the video's job.
2. Choose structure: short promo, tutorial, product intro, GitHub launch, talking head, caption video, podcast clip, or motion graphic.
3. Define hook, beats, proof, and CTA.
4. Choose visual rhythm and tool path.
5. Name missing assets and approval gates.

## Output

`video_plan`.
