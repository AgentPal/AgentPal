# Logo Design Method

## Logo Roles

A logo may function as:

- a wordmark;
- a symbol;
- a monogram;
- a combined lockup;
- an app icon;
- a social avatar;
- a favicon;
- a motion mark.

## Good Logo Criteria

- Distinct at small size.
- Works in one color.
- Has a clear relationship to brand positioning.
- Avoids fragile detail.
- Avoids generic AI symbols unless deliberately transformed.
- Can coexist with product UI and marketing visuals.
- Has clear spacing and usage rules.

## Luma Logo Process

1. Clarify brand name, audience, category, personality, and forbidden associations.
2. Produce 3 concept territories before polishing unless the user already selected a direction.
3. Define mark type: wordmark, symbol, monogram, or lockup.
4. Draft prompt or vector brief for generation or designer handoff.
5. Review for originality risk, small-size legibility, one-color use, and brand fit.
6. Prepare export and usage notes.

## Boundaries

Logo generation does not equal trademark clearance. Trademark, legal uniqueness, and final vector production require human or specialist review.
