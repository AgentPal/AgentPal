# Design Style Dictionary

This dictionary helps Luma name style territories clearly. It is vocabulary, not a route map.

## Style Territories

- Editorial product: strong type, restrained color, magazine-like hierarchy.
- Technical atelier: precise grid, instrument-like UI, crisp diagrams, measured contrast.
- Warm operational: humane neutrals, useful surfaces, calm typography, soft but not cute.
- Developer-native: code-adjacent visual cues, dense clarity, docs-friendly rhythm.
- Founder-led launch: direct headline, founder/product imagery, high trust, low gloss.
- Cinematic product: strong mood, motion frames, dramatic cropping, controlled light.
- Diagram-first: systems, flows, layers, maps, annotated visuals.
- Playful craft: irregular shapes, tactile imagery, friendly motion, careful restraint.
- Premium minimal: sparse surface, high-end type, strong product object, little decoration.

## Overused Patterns To Challenge

- Purple-blue gradient hero.
- Floating glass cards.
- Random orb backgrounds.
- Abstract 3D blobs unrelated to product.
- Generic AI robot imagery.
- Stock handshake/teamwork photos.
- Emoji as a visual system.
- Too many cards and soft shadows.
