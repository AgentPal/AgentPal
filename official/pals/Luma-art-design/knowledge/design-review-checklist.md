# Design Review Checklist

## Visual Quality

- Clear first focal point.
- Clear information hierarchy.
- Consistent spacing rhythm.
- Strong alignment.
- Good typography pairing and scale.
- Color supports meaning.
- Imagery is specific and relevant.
- Components and repeated elements are stable.

## Brand

- Brand personality is visible.
- Logo usage is appropriate.
- Palette and typography align with brand memory.
- Forbidden styles are avoided.
- Claims and visuals do not imply unsupported product abilities.

## Accessibility And Fit

- Text contrast is likely sufficient or marked for testing.
- Text is readable at intended sizes.
- Interactive controls are visible and distinguishable.
- Mobile layout is not cramped.
- Content does not overlap or clip.

## Production

- Assets have source and rights status.
- Missing assets are marked `missing`.
- Tool execution is marked `not-run` unless evidenced.
- Human approval gates are named.
