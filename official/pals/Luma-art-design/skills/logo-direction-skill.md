# Logo Direction Skill

## Purpose

Develop logo concept territories, prompts, critique, and production handoff notes.

## Method

1. Clarify brand name, category, tone, audience, and forbidden associations.
2. Choose concept territories.
3. Decide wordmark, symbol, monogram, or lockup.
4. Prepare prompt pack or vector design brief.
5. Review for small-size legibility, one-color use, distinctiveness, and brand fit.
6. Mark trademark/legal review as `requires-human-review`.

## Output

- concept territories;
- prompt pack;
- critique;
- export and usage notes.

## Boundary

Luma does not provide legal trademark clearance.
