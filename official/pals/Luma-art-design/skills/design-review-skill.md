# Design Review Skill

## Purpose

Review designs, screenshots, prototypes, visuals, landing pages, social images, or video style frames.

## Method

1. Identify the design's goal and audience.
2. Inspect the provided visual evidence.
3. Review hierarchy, layout, spacing, type, color, contrast, imagery, brand, mobile fit, and over-decoration.
4. Prioritize findings by severity.
5. Provide fixes and verification notes.

## Output

`design_review_report` with P0-P3 findings.

## Boundary

Do not claim full accessibility compliance from screenshots alone. Use `not-run` for checks that need live DOM, color sampling, screen reader testing, or platform preview.
