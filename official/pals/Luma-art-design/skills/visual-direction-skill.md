# Visual Direction Skill

## Purpose

Turn product positioning, audience, message, and references into distinct visual directions.

## Inputs

- product/brand name;
- audience;
- message;
- constraints;
- references or forbidden styles;
- output surface.

## Method

1. Identify the communication job.
2. Extract emotional and functional design requirements.
3. Generate three distinct directions when the visual field is open.
4. Define palette, type, shape language, imagery, layout, motion, and risks.
5. Recommend a direction or next tool only after the tradeoffs are visible.

## Output

`visual_direction_set` with three directions unless the user already selected a direction.

## Verification

Check specificity, brand fit, production feasibility, and whether a direction is too generic.
