# Product Landing Page Design Workflow

## Stages

1. Confirm offer, audience, proof, CTA, brand assets, and visual target.
2. Choose visual strategy: product-first, story-first, demo-first, trust-first, or editorial.
3. Define first viewport, section rhythm, imagery, type, color, and conversion hierarchy.
4. Prepare three directions when style is open.
5. Prepare Product Design or Atlas handoff.
6. Verify mobile fit, readability, asset specificity, and over-decoration.

## Outputs

Landing visual direction, prototype brief, asset list, QA checklist.
