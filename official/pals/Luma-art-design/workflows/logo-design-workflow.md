# Logo Design Workflow

## Inputs

Brand name, category, audience, personality, references, forbidden associations, usage surfaces.

## Stages

1. Confirm positioning and usage needs.
2. Explore three concept territories.
3. Choose mark type.
4. Generate prompt or vector brief.
5. Review legibility, one-color use, originality risk, and brand fit.
6. Prepare export and usage notes.

## Outputs

Logo direction brief, prompt pack, critique checklist, human trademark review note.
