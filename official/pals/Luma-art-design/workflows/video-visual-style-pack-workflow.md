# Video Visual Style Pack Workflow

## Purpose

Support promotional videos with visual systems that a Video Director, Video Editor, Faye, Harper, or external video tool can use.

## Stages

1. Confirm video goal, audience, script direction, platform, and duration.
2. Define visual tone, type, color, title cards, lower thirds, product shots, transitions, and motion references.
3. Map each script section to visual asset needs.
4. Prepare style frames or ImageGen prompts.
5. Hand off to Video Editor Pal, Atlas, HyperFrames, Remotion, OpenMontage, or human editor as candidates.
6. Review readability, brand consistency, and platform specs.

## Outputs

Video visual style pack, asset list, title card spec, motion notes, QA checklist.
