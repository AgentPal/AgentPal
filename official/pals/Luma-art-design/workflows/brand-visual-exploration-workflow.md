# Brand Visual Direction Exploration Workflow

## Stages

1. Gather product positioning, audience, tone, and references.
2. Identify visual tensions: trust vs energy, technical vs human, premium vs accessible.
3. Produce three directions with palettes, type, shapes, imagery, and sample surfaces.
4. Compare fit, risk, and production cost.
5. Recommend next step: ImageGen moodboard, Product Design ideation, Figma system, or HTML/CSS sample.

## Output

Three-direction visual exploration package.
